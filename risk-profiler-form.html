Section A — Portfolio context and horizon

Please answer the following 3 questions.

Q1. What investment time horizon do you realistically have in mind for this portfolio before you may need to access a significant portion of the money?
Q2. How important is this portfolio within your overall financial position and investment decisions?
Q3. How dependent are you on this portfolio to generate regular cash flow or income over the next 3-5 years?

Section B — Financial resilience and risk capacity

Please answer the following 3 questions.

Q4. How much liquidity do you currently maintain to address unforeseen financial needs?
Q5. How stable and predictable is your household income over the next 3-5 years?
Q6. If this portfolio were to decline materially in value, how much would that affect your financial security or core life goals?

Section C — Risk willingness and likely behavior

Please answer the following 4 questions.

Q7. In order to pursue higher returns, how much investment risk are you willing to take with this portfolio?
Q8. If a few months after investing, the value of your portfolio declines by about 20% due to market movements, what would you most likely do?
Q9. If this portfolio underperformed expectations for 2-3 years but remained aligned with the long-term plan, what would you most likely do?
Q10. Imagine you are participating in a game show and must choose one of the following options. Which option would you most likely select?

Q11. How would you describe your familiarity with investing in financial markets?
